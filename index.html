Currently Under Review

Mapping tidal erosion along the Sundarbans coastline

A twenty-two month field study tracking sediment loss across four monitoring stations, conducted with local fishing communities as long-term observers rather than one-time subjects. Early findings suggest erosion rates accelerate non-linearly near mangrove root systems damaged by upstream dredging.
