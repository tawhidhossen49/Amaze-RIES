Join

Open to anyone who can defend their work.

We don't ask what university you attended or how old you are. We ask what question you're trying to answer, and how you'd know if you were wrong.

Researcher

Leads a proposal through the full four-stage process.

Expected: one active study every 18 months.

Associate

Serves as a specialist or generalist reviewer on others' work.

Expected: two reviews per year.

Affiliate

Contributes data, fieldwork, or translation support to active studies.

Expected: as agreed per project.

Student

Shadows a Researcher on an active study for one full cycle.

Expected: attendance at working sessions.

How It Works

Four steps, six to eight weeks.

01

Apply

Submit a short written statement — no CV required, though you may include one.

02

Interview

A 30-minute conversation with two current members, focused entirely on how you think through a problem.

03

Trial Review

Review one existing study as a test case. We're checking whether you can find a real flaw, not whether you're polite about it.

04

Confirmed

Membership is confirmed and you're assigned to your first active review or study.
