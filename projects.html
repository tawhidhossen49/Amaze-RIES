Case Study

Failure modes in municipal water systems

Eleven months into a field survey across three municipal water networks, the team has documented forty-two distinct failure events. The pattern emerging so far: failures cluster around administrative handoffs between maintenance shifts, not around equipment age, which was the initial hypothesis.

Current phase: cross-referencing failure logs against staffing schedules.
